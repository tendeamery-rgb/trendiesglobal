Trendies Global

Public website: https://trendiesglobal.com/
Founder/contact: Tende Amery, tende.amery@gmail.com

Trendies Global is an idea-stage coming-of-age summer community hub for people who want to make new friends, expand their circle, pin their country, leave a wall note and shape Chapter One together.

Key themes: coming-of-age summer, Chapter One, safe public plans, picnics, bike rides, live music, card games, short films, exploring somewhere new, making new friends and expanding your circle.
